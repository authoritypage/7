Foundational Doctrine

The Ministry's core mandate is the preservation of economic and digital sovereignty against coercive legal tactics. Our primary analytical framework is derived from the principle of the "Blackmail Effect," as articulated in foundational jurisprudence.

The blackmail effect of class actions involves "forcing... defendants to stake their companies on the outcome of a single jury trial, or be forced by fear of the risk of bankruptcy to settle even if they have no legal liability."
